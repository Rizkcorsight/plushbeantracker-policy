## Plain-language summary

Plush Bean Tracker collects nothing. Your collection, your photos,
your ratings, and your purchase log live on your device. There are
no servers, no accounts, no logins. The app does not talk to
advertisers, analytics services, or any third-party data processor.
Plush Bean Tracker is a one-time paid app — no subscription, no
ads, no in-app purchases, no tracking.

## What's stored on your device

- **Your collection list** — every plush you add (name, category,
  status of Owned/Wanted/Trading, notes) is saved as a small local
  preferences blob in your app's private storage.
- **Your photos** — photos you attach to any plush are stored as
  files inside the app's private App Group container. They are
  encoded as JPEG and named by a random identifier. Photos never
  leave your device.
- **Cuddle ratings** — your 1–5 ratings per plush are stored locally.
- **Birth-certificate notes** — the name and wish you write for
  each plush are stored locally as plain text.
- **Purchase log** — for each plush, the dates, prices paid, source
  channels (Physical store / Online store / Mystery pack / Gift /
  Resale), and free-text notes you enter are stored locally.
- **Onboarding completion flag** — a single boolean so the welcome
  screens don't show on every launch.
- **Widget snapshot** — a tiny local summary (total owned, total
  spent, mystery-pull count) is written to the shared App Group
  container so the home-screen widget can render without launching
  the app. This snapshot also stays on your device.

## What's NOT collected

- No name. No email. No phone number. No location. No birthdate.
- No advertising identifier. No analytics. No crash reporter.
- No microphone, contacts, calendar, health, or fitness access.
- No internet connection is made by the app for any feature.
  (Photo picker, Files import, and Share Sheet are system-provided
  interactions; data is only what the user explicitly selects.)
- We do not collect, sell, share, or rent any data — because we
  do not have any data to collect, sell, share, or rent.

## Camera and photo library

The app asks for camera and photo library access only when you tap
"Add your own photo" on a plush. iOS shows you the system permission
prompt. If you allow access, any photo you pick is copied into the
app's private App Group container and tagged with a random identifier.
The original photo in your photo library is not modified or deleted.

If you deny access or never tap "Add your own photo," no camera or
photo data is read.

## Backup and Restore (Export to Files)

The app's "Backup & restore" feature lets you export your entire
collection — every plush, photo, and purchase record — to a single
`.plushbean` JSON file. This file is generated locally and shown to
you via the system share sheet (Files, AirDrop, Mail, Messages,
etc.). The destination is entirely your choice. We never see the
file. Re-importing it on the same or another device restores your
collection in place.

## Third-party services

There are none. Plush Bean Tracker does not embed any third-party
analytics SDK, advertising SDK, attribution SDK, or telemetry SDK.
The app's Privacy Manifest (`PrivacyInfo.xcprivacy`) declares no
data collection and no tracking. Apple's App Privacy nutrition
label reflects "Data Not Collected."

## Children's privacy

Plush Bean Tracker is rated 4+ and contains no objectionable
content, ads, or in-app purchases. Because we collect no data
whatsoever, we collect no data from children. The app is suitable
for all ages.

## Your rights

Because the app collects no data and stores all data on your device,
exercising rights under GDPR, CCPA, LGPD, or any similar regulation
is straightforward:

- **Right to access**: you have full access — open the app.
- **Right to deletion**: delete the app or use the in-app delete
  per plush. Deleting the app removes all stored data including
  photos from your device.
- **Right to portability**: use the in-app "Backup & restore" to
  export everything as a portable JSON file.
- **Right to opt out of sale**: not applicable — nothing is sold,
  shared, or transmitted.

## App Store purchases

The one-time $1.99 purchase is processed by Apple's App Store.
Plush Bean Tracker does not receive or store your payment card
details. Refer to Apple's privacy policy for App Store transactions:
<https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/>
